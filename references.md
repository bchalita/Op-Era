# References

Research papers that informed Op-Era's design and methodology.

## Foundational Systems

- **OptiMUS: Scalable Optimization Modeling with (MI)LP Solvers and Large Language Models**
  AhmadiTeshnizi, Gao, Laradji (2024). The baseline system Op-Era extends.
  [arXiv:2402.10172](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.10172)

- **OptimAI: Optimization from Natural Language Using LLM-Powered AI Agents**
  Google DeepMind (2025). Multi-agent approach to optimization problem solving.
  [arXiv:2502.06758](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.06758)

## LLM + Optimization

- **AutoOpt: Automatic Optimization Problem Formulation from Natural Language**
  Huang et al. (2024). End-to-end NL-to-formulation pipeline.
  [arXiv:2404.05849](https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.05849)

- **EvoOpt: Evolutionary Optimization of LLM-Generated Solutions**
  Multi-round refinement of LLM-generated optimization code.
  [arXiv:2504.06538](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.06538)

- **An LLM-Powered MILP Modelling Engine**
  Formal verification approach to LLM-generated mathematical programs.
  [arXiv:2407.09571](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.09571)

- **An LLM-Based Modeling Assistant for EV Charging Optimization**
  Domain-specific application of LLM-assisted optimization modeling.
  [arXiv:2501.07654](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.07654)
